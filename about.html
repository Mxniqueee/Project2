Hey there, bud!

My name is Monique Arao Garcia and most of my friends call me "Momo". I immigrated here from the Philippines with my family in 2002, when we landed here the day I turned six years old. Ever since I moved here, I naturally gravitated towards computers and technology in general, and my first step to this was through Neopets!

I am double majoring in Computer Science and Web Development, but I'm thinking about majoring in Web Development solely, because it combines Computer Science with my love for Graphic Design. I have been doing some freelance graphic design ever since my sophomore year of high school, and I did this more intensively in the last school year, when I was the Graphic Designer for the Associated Student Organization (ASO). In this job, I worked on flyers for events that the ASO hosted, as well as Snapchat Geofilters.
